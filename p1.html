Computer programming language, any of various languages for expressing a set of detailed instructions for a digital computer. Such instructions

can be executed directly when they are in the computer manufacturer-specific numerical form known as machine language, after a

simple substitution process when expressed in a corresponding assembly language, or after translation from some “higher-level”

language. Although there are many computer languages, relatively few are widely used.
